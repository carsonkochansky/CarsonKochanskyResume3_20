About

A little about me

I'm a senior at Bronxville High School with a passion for science, research, and athletics. My academic interests span space biology, genetics, quantum mechanics, psychology, and artificial intelligence — and I love finding the connections between them.

This past year I had the incredible opportunity to intern at NASA's Ames Research Center through the GL4HS program, analyzing RNA sequencing data from space-flown organisms. I also shadowed cancer research at NYU Langone's Tisch Hospital.

Outside the lab and classroom, I'm a varsity soccer player and track athlete, president of both the Astronomy and Chess clubs, a volunteer English teacher with TASSEL in Cambodia, and a photographer whose work has appeared in local newspapers.

I'll be attending Dartmouth College in the fall of 2026 as part of the Class of 2030.
